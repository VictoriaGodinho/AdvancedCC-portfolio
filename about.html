ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Victoria Godinho, and I’m pursuing a degree in Design and Technology at Parsons School of Design in New York City. My work lives at the intersection of UX design, storytelling, and creative coding.

I design and develop websites, apps, and interactive experiences that are both intuitive and visually engaging. With a background in art and illustration, I bring a strong visual sensibility into my projects, combining creative expression with technical execution. I enjoy working with Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, and Unity, as well as coding in JavaScript, HTML, and CSS.

Having lived in three different countries, I’ve learned to see the world through multiple perspectives. This experience shapes how I approach design and technology, as ways to connect cultures, tell stories, and build bridges between people. My projects often reflect this mix of influences.
